User Interaction Features

Manage how you interact with 
@contextdogs
Follow?

Posts, likes, comments, and reposts will appear on your feed with a higher priority and regularity.

Mute?

Posts, likes, comments, and reposts will not appear on your feed or come through notifications.

Block?

Blocked users can no longer directly interact with any of your content, whether it be liking, commenting, or reposting. Soon, blocked users will be able to view your public posts regardless.
