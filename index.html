James Orson - X - HCI Prototypes

Thanks for looking at my Human-Computer Interaction prototypes! I have decided to consider a redesign of how to handle user interactions on social media platforms like X. Below are two prototypes for alternative views to display this to the user. These prototypes are based on the current circumstance where you are looking at the settings for someone you already follow, and do not mute or block.

The first is a "descriptors" design, being a novel design.

The second is a "feature" design, using the existing concepts of the platform.
